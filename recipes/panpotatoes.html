Pan Fried Potatoes

Description

Pan fried potatoes are a delicious addition to any breakfast, lunch or dinner. Not only are they tasty, but they're healthy and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs salt
  • 1 lbs red potato
    • 1 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 3 tbs olive oil

Steps

  1. Using a mandoline or knife slice potatoes into 1/3 inch slices
  2. Place potatoes in a large bowl, add 2 tbsp olive oil and salt.
  3. Mix until potatoes are coated evenly
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and coat pan with remaining olive oil
  5. Place potato slices in pan in a single layer
  6. Let potatoes cook for 5 minutes, flip and let cook for another 5 minutes
  7. Remove potatoes from heat and place back in bowl
  8. Add garlic powder and black pepper, toss until evenly coated
  9. Serve & enjoy!
