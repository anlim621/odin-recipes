Peach Cobbler

Description

Peach Cobbler is a very easy and delicious dessert to make. Total prep time is only about 15 minutes, and after 40 minutes of baking your dessert will be ready to eat! All you need is fresh peaches and you probably have the recipes sitting in your pantry!

Ingredients

  • 5 peaches
  • 1 3/4 cups sugar
    • 5 tablespoons salted butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 3/4 cups milk
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder

Steps

  1. Boil peaches in boiling water for 30 seconds each and quickly transfer to ice bath- this makes it easy to remove skin
  2. Skin and slice peaches placing them in medium saucepan
  3. Add 3/4 cups sugar to peaches and cook over medum heat just until sugar is released from peaches
  4. Move peaches off heat
  5. Take 6 tbs butter and place in 13x9in baking tray
  6. Place baking tray in oven and preheat oven to 350 deg F
  7. While oven is preheating, grab a mixing bowl and add flour, remaining sugar, and baking powder
  8. Mix flour, sugar and baking powder well
  9. Add milk to mixing bowl and continue to mix until no flour clumps remain
  10. Once oven is preheated remove tray and add dough mixture to tray
  11. Add peaches ontop of dough mixture
  12. Place dish back in oven and bake for 40 minutes
  13. Let cobbler sit for 10 minutes to cool
  14. Serve with vanilla ice cream and enjoy!
