Cast Iron Pizza

Description

Are you scared of making pizza at home? If so, not to worry a lot of people are just like you! This pizza recipe is great because it is simple and makes handling the dough as easy as possible. No anxiety about tossing it here!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet Fleischmann'sPizza Crust Yeast
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup Pizza sauce
  • Toppings of your choice
  • 6 oz low-moisture mozzerella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 deg with rack in bottom 1/3 of oven
  2. Combine flour, yeast, and salt in a large bowl
  3. Mix bowl contents until well blended
  4. Add warm water and 1 tbs olive oil to bowl and mix until soft dough is formed
  5. Knead dough until a soft elastic ball is formed (should only take a few minutes)
  6. Add remaining 1 tbs of olive oil into 10 inch cast iron skillet
  7. Transfer doughball to skillet and use ball to coat skillet with olive oil
  8. Flatten dough to edges of skillet
  9. Use 3 oz of mozzerella cheese and coat dough
  10. Add pizza sauce a top first layer of cheese
  11. Add remaining cheese on top of sauce
  12. Add toppings as desired
  13. Place cast iron in oven and bake for 30 minutes or until crust is golden brown
  14. Remove cast iron from oven and let cool on heat proof surface for 10 minutes- CAREFUL PAN WILL BE HOT
  15. Slice and enjoy!
